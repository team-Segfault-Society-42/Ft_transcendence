# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are a team of five students working on the 42 school project **ft_transcendence**.

We want this repository to remain a respectful, collaborative, and constructive space for every team member and contributor.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions, reviews, and comments.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the code and the project.
- Ask questions in good faith and help teammates when possible.
- Keep communication professional and solution-oriented.
- Respect the workflows and decisions agreed on by the team.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or hostile language.
- Mocking teammates’ questions, mistakes, or work.
- Repeatedly ignoring team rules or review feedback.
- Sharing sensitive information such as secrets, credentials, or private data.

## Enforcement

If a contributor behaves in a way that harms the team or the project, the team may discuss the issue and take appropriate action.

## Contact

For conduct-related concerns, contact the team privately through the usual team communication channel.
